## Details 

Small shrub/woody herbaceous plant.

Leaves 
- Alternately arranged
- 3-13cm long 
- Mostly hairless
- Pointed tips
- Ovate or oblong

Flowers 
- Whitish arranged in elongated clusters (4-8cm long)
- Sometimes pinkish tinged
- four small petals 1-3mm long and four stamens

## Control 

- Complete physical removal.
- Foliar spray
